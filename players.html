DNDLinks

Player Resources

whothefuckismydndcharacter

Who The Fuck is My DND charaecter is a site that simply gives you a randomy generated race, demeanor class and backstory. It's great for inspiration for your next charaecter.

RPGBot

RPG Bot is an analysis and review site for all of the classes and subclasses in Dungeons and Dragons. It's a great way to get a good idea of what is good for a charaecter, and what is bad along with a nice summary of what the subclass does and does not excel at.

DND Beyond

The offical digital toolset for Dungeons and Dragons 5e. The site has some really nice and easy to use charaecter building tools and a built in dice roller.

5e.tools

n unoffical digital toolset that focuses on having pretty much everything available. It's a lot tougher to use than DND Beyond, but its a lot more complete and more focused towards power gamers.

Critical Role

A long running DND twitch.tv series that follows the adventures of a bunch of professional voice actors. This show is extremly high quality!

Dimension 20

A Youtube/Dropout.tv series that has a large cast of different players in wacky situations ran by a common dungeon master. This series has fully designed sets to go along with the fighting.

NADDPOD

A sister podcast to Dimension 20, this podcast follows a gang of strange and incompetent heroes as they struggle to do the right thing.
